ABOUT

Sunita Defence Inc. is a strategic partner to the U.S. aerospace and defense sectors. As a wholly-owned subsidiary of Sunita Tools Ltd. (est. 1988), we bridge 35 years of manufacturing excellence with targeted domestic acquisitions.

By integrating the precision machining of our parent company with the specialized manufacturing of New Mold Innovations, we offer a unified supply chain for mission-critical hardware and protective systems.

From our headquarters in Chicago to our strategic assets in Kentucky, we are built to deliver compliance, scale, and performance.

CAPABILITIES

Precision Artillery & Munitions

We specialize in the production of 155mm M107 empty shells and various caliber defense components. Our heritage in high-volume CNC machining ensures absolute reliability for government tenders and international defense contracts.

Aerospace Precision Machining

We are India's leading manufacturer of Precision Component and Pocket Machining, machining parts for the Aerospace and Defense parts such as Missile Nose, Cockpit Structure, Oil and Gas Valves, Machine Beds and Base.

CNC Machining

As pioneers of the industry in India, we offer Precision CNC and Precision Finish CNC Machining. Our facility utilizes state-of-the-art 5-axis CNC machining centers to produce mission-critical parts with micron-level tolerances.

Mold Bases

Our Molds and Bases product range include a wide range of Custom Mould Bases, Plastic Mould Bases, Mould Bases For Caps & Closures, Standard Mould Bases and Die Casting Mould Bases.

No-Leak Grease Cartridge

New Mold Innovations is a vertically integrated grease and cartridge platform. We manufacture and fill our patented plastic cartridges — delivering a cleaner, faster, and more reliable alternative to traditional grease tubes.
